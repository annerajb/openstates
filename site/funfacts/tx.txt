The term "Six Flags over Texas" stems from the six nations that had ruled over this state&mdash;Spain, France, Mexico, United States, The Confederacy, and Texas' time as an independent republic.

Texas' official state small mammal is the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armadillo">armor-plated armadillo.</a>

Texas' state snack is Tortilla Chips and Salsa.

The state molecule of Texas is the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fullerene">buckyball</a>.

Texas was admitted into the United States in 1845, left in 1861 and was readmitted in 1870.

The Texas state fruit is the Red Grapefuit. Awesome.

The official Texas flying mammal is the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mexican_Free-tailed_Bat">Mexican free-tailed bat</a>.

The Texas state flag is the only US state flag that was one the flag of an independent country.
