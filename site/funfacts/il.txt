
The Illinois state snack is...popcorn! There are 333 popcorn farms in Illinois growing 47,000 acres of popcorn each year.

Illinois was the first state to ratify the 13th Amendment, which officially outlaws slavery and involuntary servitude.

The name Illinois stems from the Illiniwek Confederation of tribes that controlled the region.

The Willis (Sears) tower in Chicago is the tallest building in the United States.

There are over 6,000 units of government (city, township, county, etc.) in Illinois.
